From Science Class to Digital Marketing Expert

My journey has been anything but ordinary. I began in Science at Bogura YMCA Public School, completed my HSC in Commerce from Govt. Shah Sultan College, and I'm currently pursuing a BBA Honours in Management at Govt. Azizul Haque College — each step sharpening my analytical and strategic mindset.

That diverse academic foundation led me to discover my true passion: Digital Marketing. I completed a rigorous 3-month program under the Learning and Earning Development Project (LEDP), earning expertise in SEO, Social Media Marketing, and Content Strategy — skills I now use to help businesses grow in the digital space.

I believe in data-driven creativity — crafting campaigns that don't just look good, but deliver measurable results. Whether you need organic search growth, a stronger social presence, or compelling content, I'm ready to make it happen.
